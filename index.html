Our 5 Main Programs

From Crisis to Growth

Each program moves families from crisis to stability, and from stability to long-term growth.

🎓
Education & Tuition Centres
After-school tuition, exam coaching, stationery & school supplies. Many students are the first in their family to reach higher grades.
  • Penpal Program (Canada ↔ Sri Lanka)
  • One-to-One Sponsorship
  • 11+ Tuition Centres active
🧵
Skills Development
Practical sewing training and computer education — creating sustainable livelihoods and independence for women across Northern Sri Lanka.
  • Sewing Training Centres
  • 2 Sewing Stores live
  • Women Empowerment Workshops
🌱
Business Development & Micro-Loans
Repayable micro-loans and structured follow-up to help families start and grow income-generating businesses.
  • 100+ businesses supported
  • Jobs created tracking
  • Revolving loan fund
👩
Women Empowerment Projects
300+ women's groups of 5–15 members receive Rs. 2,500 seed funds for micro-enterprises — palmyra products, candles, soap, and more.
  • Women's Economic Empowerment Fund
  • Some groups grown to Rs. 10 Lakhs
  • Mannar, Vanni & Mullaithivu
🏡
Family Support & Care
Food assistance, medical supplies, housing & washroom construction, bicycles for students, eyeglasses. Supporting Maveerar families and disaster relief.
  • Mullaitivu, Mannar, Vavuniya
  • Malayagam communities
  • Emergency & disaster response
